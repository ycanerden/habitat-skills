## Technical Specs

### Video Settings
- **Duration:** 18-20 seconds max
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (or 1080x1920 for vertical/social)
- **Format:** MP4, H.264 codec
- **Frame rate:** 30fps or 60fps

### Editing Notes
- **Speed:** Screen recordings at 1.5x-2x speed (make it feel fast)
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts, no fades (keep energy high)
- **Text:** Bold, sans-serif font (Inter or SF Pro)
- **Colors:** Clean, minimal palette (black, white, one accent color)
- **Music:** Upbeat, tech-y background track (low volume, no lyrics)

### Subtitles/Captions
Add text overlays for each shot (see above). No voiceover needed‚Äîtext only.

### Export Versions
1. **Horizontal (16:9):** For presentations, YouTube
2. **Vertical (9:16):** For Instagram, TikTok, Stories
3. **Square (1:1):** For LinkedIn, Twitter
